- **Legacy Infrastructure:** Converting 20-year-old COBOL or monolithic Java into modern microservices.
- **Predictive DevOps:** Generating entire integration test suites and Terraform plans from high-level architecture sketches.
- **Complex Compliance:** Auditing thousands of VPCs or 5,000-page legal contracts for hidden risks.
